Our Story

Hi there, I'm Yeshashree. My partner Charan and I absolutely love watching documentary movies and series. We also love talking about it, reflecting, arguing and researching new pieces of information we learned after watching a documentary together. I enjoy documentaries about climbing and mountaineering, biopics and popular culture. Charan loves anything related to sports in general and conspiracy theories (although he does not like to admit it).

The idea for this website was born out of a converstation we had almost a year ago sitting on a bench at the park. We realized that we wanted an avanue to create something out of our discussions about the films we watched that would benifit the larger documentary fan community. Keeping track of what we watched and what we want to watch was getting hard for us. I wanted to also create curated lists to help people choose what they're interested in. So we decided to build a website which aims to do just this. Our friends often contribute their reviews to the blog. You can read about them below.

If you are interested in contributing your reviews, lists or any other content about documentaries please reach out to us on Facebook. We usually reply within 24 hours :)
